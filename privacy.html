Privacy Policy

App: Vooper
Last updated: March 5, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Vooper ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use the Vooper mobile application (the "App"). Vooper provides a visual dashboard for your fitness and recovery data retrieved from WHOOP with your authorization.

1. Information We Collect

Depending on how you use the App, we may collect the following types of information:

  • WHOOP account data: data you authorize through WHOOP APIs (for example: recovery, strain, sleep, workouts, and related metrics).
  • Authentication data: OAuth tokens or similar credentials required to connect your WHOOP account.
  • Basic device/app data: app version, device type, operating system, and diagnostic logs for stability and troubleshooting.
  • Contact information: if you email support, we collect the email address and message content you provide.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To connect to WHOOP and display your data inside the App.
  • To provide analytics, trends, and visual insights based on your authorized data.
  • To maintain and improve app performance, reliability, and features.
  • To respond to support requests and communicate important service updates.
  • To comply with legal obligations.

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We may share data only in the following limited situations:

  • With service providers that help us operate the App (for example, cloud hosting, analytics, or crash reporting), under confidentiality obligations.
  • If required by law, regulation, legal process, or valid government request.
  • To protect the rights, safety, and security of users, our services, or others.

4. Data Retention

We keep personal data only for as long as necessary for the purposes described in this Privacy Policy, unless a longer retention period is required by law. You may request account and associated data deletion by contacting us.

5. Data Security

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational safeguards to protect your data. However, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure.

6. Your Choices and Rights

  • You can disconnect WHOOP access at any time through the App and/or your WHOOP account settings.
  • You can request access, correction, or deletion of your personal data by contacting us.
  • You can stop using the App at any time and uninstall it from your device.

7. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed to children under 13 (or the relevant minimum age in your jurisdiction). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

8. Third-Party Services

The App integrates with WHOOP and may use third-party infrastructure services. Use of those services is subject to their own privacy policies and terms.

9. International Data Transfers

If you use the App outside the country where our service providers are located, your information may be transferred to and processed in other countries.

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make material changes, we will update the "Last updated" date and, where appropriate, provide additional notice.
